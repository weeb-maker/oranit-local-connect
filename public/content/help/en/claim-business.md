## Why claim your business?

Claiming your business listing gives you:

- **Full control** over your business information
- Ability to **respond to reviews** and engage with customers
- **Post deals and specials** to attract more customers
- **Upload photos** and showcase your business
- Access to **analytics** about your listing's performance
- **Verification badge** showing you're the official owner

## Eligibility

To claim a business, you must:

- Be the legal owner or authorized representative
- Have access to business contact information (phone/email)
- Provide proof of ownership (business license, utility bill, etc.)

## Step-by-step process

### 1. Find your business

- Search for your business on Oranit.biz
- Navigate to your business listing page
- If your business isn't listed yet, you can add it first

### 2. Click "Claim this business"

- Look for the **Claim this business** button on your listing
- You'll need to be signed in to your Oranit.biz account
- If you don't have an account, create one first

### 3. Verify your identity

Choose one of the verification methods:

**Phone verification:**
- We'll send a code to your business phone number
- Enter the code to verify you have access

**Email verification:**
- We'll send a verification link to your business email
- Click the link to confirm ownership

**Document upload:**
- Upload a business license, utility bill, or official document
- Our team will review and approve within 24-48 hours

### 4. Complete your profile

Once verified:

- Fill in all business details (hours, services, contact info)
- Upload high-quality photos
- Add a compelling description
- Set up your first deal or special offer

## Verification timeline

- **Instant:** Phone/email verification (within minutes)
- **Document review:** 24-48 hours during business days
- You'll receive an email notification once approved

## Troubleshooting

**Can't find your business?**
- Try different search terms or spellings
- Check if your business is listed under a different name
- If still not found, add it as a new listing first

**Verification code not received?**
- Check spam/junk folders
- Verify the contact information is correct
- Try the alternative verification method

**Already claimed by someone else?**
- Contact our support team with proof of ownership
- We'll investigate and transfer the listing if appropriate
