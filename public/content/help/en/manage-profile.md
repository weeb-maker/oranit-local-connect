## Your Profile on Oranit.biz

Your profile helps neighbors and businesses recognize you. Keep it up to date to make the most of the community directory.

## Edit Your Details

1. Go to **Settings → Profile** from the navigation menu
2. Update your profile photo
3. Edit your display name and bio
4. Add or change contact information
5. Click **Save Changes**

## Privacy Settings

You have full control over what information is visible:

- **Public Profile:** Choose which details appear to other users
- **Contact Visibility:** Decide if businesses can reach you
- **Review Attribution:** Control whether your name appears on reviews

## Deactivate Your Account

If you need a break:

1. Go to **Settings → Privacy**
2. Click **Deactivate Account**
3. Your profile will be temporarily hidden
4. You can reactivate anytime by signing in again

## Need Help?

If you have questions about privacy or account settings, contact our support team via the **Contact** page.
