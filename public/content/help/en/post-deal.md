## Promote Your Business with Deals

Deals and specials are a great way to attract new customers and reward loyal ones in the Oranit community.

## Prerequisites

Before posting a deal:

1. You must have claimed your business listing
2. Your business profile should be complete with photos
3. Sign in to your business account

## Creating a Deal

1. Go to your business dashboard
2. Click **Deals → Add New Deal**
3. Fill in the required information

## Deal Details

Provide these details:

**Basic Information:**
- **Title:** Clear, catchy headline (e.g., "20% Off All Pizzas")
- **Description:** Explain what's included and any terms
- **Category:** Select the deal type (Discount, BOGO, Special Offer, etc.)

**Pricing:**
- **Original Price:** Regular price (optional)
- **Deal Price:** Discounted price
- **Discount Percentage:** Will be calculated automatically

**Validity:**
- **Start Date:** When the deal becomes active
- **End Date:** When the deal expires
- **Days/Hours:** Specify if it's valid on certain days only

**Images:**
- Upload at least one high-quality photo
- Use images that showcase the product or service

## Tips for Successful Deals

- **Be Clear:** Specify any restrictions or requirements
- **Create Urgency:** Use limited-time offers
- **Highlight Value:** Show the savings clearly
- **Use Great Photos:** Visual appeal matters
- **Update Regularly:** Keep deals fresh and relevant

## Managing Active Deals

- View all your deals from your dashboard
- Edit or extend deals before they expire
- Mark deals as "Featured" to increase visibility
- Track performance with analytics

## Deal Guidelines

Follow these rules:

- Deals must be genuine and accurate
- Honor all terms and conditions you set
- Don't create misleading promotions
- Respond to customer inquiries promptly

## Need Help?

Contact business support at business@oranit.biz for assistance with creating effective deals.
