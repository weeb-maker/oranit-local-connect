## Your Privacy Matters

At Oranit.biz, we're committed to protecting your personal information and giving you control over your data.

## What Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

**Account Information:**
- Name and email address
- Profile photo (optional)
- Phone number (optional)

**Usage Data:**
- Pages you visit on Oranit.biz
- Searches you perform
- Businesses you save or review
- Analytics and performance data

**Business Data (for business accounts):**
- Business name, address, and contact details
- Photos and descriptions you upload
- Reviews and responses

## How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us:

- Provide and improve our services
- Personalize your experience
- Send important updates and notifications
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data:

**Access:** View all data we have about you
**Correction:** Update or fix inaccurate information
**Deletion:** Request permanent deletion of your account and data
**Portability:** Download your data in a portable format
**Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from marketing communications

## Managing Your Privacy

**From Your Profile Settings:**

1. Go to **Settings → Privacy**
2. Choose what information is visible to others
3. Control who can contact you
4. Manage email preferences
5. Request data export or deletion

**Public vs Private Information:**

**Always Public:**
- Reviews you write (with your display name)
- Business listings you create (as business owner)

**You Choose:**
- Profile photo visibility
- Contact information visibility
- Activity history visibility

**Always Private:**
- Email address (unless you choose to display it)
- Password (encrypted and never visible)
- Payment information (if applicable)

## Data Security

We protect your information with:

- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Servers:** Hosted on trusted, certified infrastructure
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access with strict protocols
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and vulnerability testing

## Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience:

**Essential Cookies:** Required for the site to function (login, language preference)
**Analytics Cookies:** Help us understand how you use the site
**Marketing Cookies:** Remember your preferences for targeted content

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

## Third-Party Sharing

We **do not** sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

- **Service Providers:** Only what's needed to operate the platform (hosting, analytics)
- **Business Owners:** When you contact them through our platform
- **Legal Authorities:** Only when required by law

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.

## Children's Privacy

Oranit.biz is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive Accounts:** Deleted after 2 years of inactivity (with notice)
- **Reviews:** Kept indefinitely for community benefit (can be deleted individually)
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer for compliance

## Your Responsibilities

Help us protect your privacy:

- Keep your password secure and unique
- Don't share personal information in public reviews
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Review privacy settings regularly

## Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy practices. When we do:

- We'll notify you via email
- Changes will be posted on our Privacy page
- Continued use means acceptance of updates

## Questions or Concerns?

Contact our privacy team:

- Email: privacy@oranit.biz
- Visit: [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy) for full details
- Request: Data access or deletion from your settings

## Reporting Privacy Issues

If you believe your privacy has been compromised:

1. Contact us immediately at privacy@oranit.biz
2. Provide details of the concern
3. We'll investigate and respond within 72 hours

Your trust is important to us, and we take privacy concerns very seriously.
