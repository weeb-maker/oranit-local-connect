## Welcome to Oranit.biz

Oranit.biz is your local directory for discovering businesses, deals, and community events in the Oranit area.

## Create your profile

1. Click the **Sign In** button in the top right corner
2. Choose to sign up with email or social login
3. Fill in your basic information
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile with a photo and bio (optional)

## Set your language preference

Oranit.biz is fully bilingual:

- Click the language switcher in the navigation bar (EN/עב)
- Select English or Hebrew
- Your preference is saved automatically
- All content adapts to your chosen language with proper RTL/LTR layout

## Explore the directory

Once you're set up, you can:

- **Browse businesses** by category or location
- **Search** for specific services or products
- **View deals** and special offers from local merchants
- **Check events** happening in the community
- **Read reviews** from other residents
- **Save favorites** to quickly access businesses you love
