## Add Your Business to Oranit.biz

List your business in the local directory to reach more customers in the Oranit community.

## Getting Started

1. Sign in to your Oranit.biz account
2. Click the **Add Business** button from your dashboard
3. If you don't have an account yet, create one first

## Required Information

Fill in these essential details:

- **Business Name:** Your official business name
- **Category:** Choose the most relevant category (e.g., Restaurant, Salon, Services)
- **Address:** Full street address in Oranit
- **Phone Number:** Primary contact number
- **Email:** Business email address

## Optional Details

Enhance your listing with:

- **Website:** Link to your site
- **Hours:** Operating hours for each day
- **Description:** Tell customers about your business
- **Photos:** Upload high-quality images (recommended: at least 3 photos)
- **Social Media:** Add links to your social profiles

## After Submission

- Your listing will be reviewed within 24-48 hours
- You'll receive an email notification once approved
- You can then claim ownership to unlock premium features

## Tips for Better Visibility

- Upload clear, professional photos
- Write a detailed description with relevant keywords
- Keep your hours and contact info up to date
- Respond to customer reviews promptly

## Need Help?

Contact our business support team at business@oranit.biz for assistance with creating your listing.
