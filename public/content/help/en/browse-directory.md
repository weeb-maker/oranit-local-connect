## Finding businesses on Oranit.biz

Our directory makes it easy to discover local businesses, services, and professionals in the Oranit area.

## Search methods

### Quick search
- Use the search bar at the top of any page
- Type business names, categories, or services
- Results appear instantly as you type
- Supports both English and Hebrew

### Browse by category
- Click **Explore** in the navigation menu
- Choose from organized categories like:
  - Restaurants & Cafes
  - Health & Beauty
  - Home Services
  - Professional Services
  - Shopping & Retail
  - And many more...

### Featured businesses
- Check the homepage for highlighted businesses
- Featured listings are carefully curated
- Updated regularly to showcase different businesses

## Filters and sorting

Refine your search with filters:

**Location:**
- Filter by neighborhood or distance
- See businesses closest to you first

**Rating:**
- Sort by highest rated
- Filter by minimum star rating

**Hours:**
- Find businesses open now
- Filter by weekend hours

**Price range:**
- Filter by budget (₪, ₪₪, ₪₪₪)

**Amenities:**
- Parking available
- Wheelchair accessible
- Outdoor seating
- Free Wi-Fi

## Business listings

Each listing shows:

- **Business name** and category
- **Star rating** and review count
- **Address** and map location
- **Phone number** and website
- **Hours of operation**
- **Photos** of the business
- **Special badges** (verified, new, featured)

## Viewing details

Click any business to see:

- Full description and services offered
- Complete photo gallery
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Current deals and specials
- Location map with directions
- Contact options (call, email, website)

## Saving favorites

- Click the heart icon on any listing
- Access your saved businesses from your profile
- Get notified about new deals from favorites

## Tips for better results

- Be specific in your search (e.g., "Italian restaurant" vs "food")
- Use filters to narrow down options
- Check "Open now" to see currently available businesses
- Read reviews to learn from other customers' experiences
- Look for the verification badge for confirmed businesses

## Mobile experience

The directory is fully optimized for mobile:

- Fast search results
- One-tap to call or get directions
- Swipeable photo galleries
- Location-aware recommendations

## Need help?

Can't find what you're looking for? Try:
- Adjusting your search terms
- Removing some filters
- Browsing the full category list
- Contacting us to suggest a business addition
