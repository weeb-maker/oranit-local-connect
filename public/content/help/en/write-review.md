## Why Reviews Matter

Your reviews help neighbors make informed decisions and support quality local businesses in Oranit.

## How to Write a Review

1. **Find the Business:** Search or browse to the business page
2. **Click Write a Review:** Look for the review button on the business profile
3. **Sign In:** You must be logged in to post reviews
4. **Rate & Write:** Give a star rating and share your experience

## Star Rating Guide

- **5 Stars:** Excellent – Exceeded expectations
- **4 Stars:** Good – Met expectations
- **3 Stars:** Average – Acceptable with room for improvement
- **2 Stars:** Below Average – Didn't meet expectations
- **1 Star:** Poor – Very unsatisfied

## Writing Helpful Reviews

**Be Specific:**
- Mention what service or product you used
- Include details about your visit (date, occasion)
- Describe what went well or poorly

**Be Honest:**
- Share genuine experiences
- Balance positive and negative feedback
- Avoid exaggeration

**Be Respectful:**
- Use professional language
- Focus on facts, not personal attacks
- Give constructive feedback

**Be Relevant:**
- Review the business, not the platform
- Avoid unrelated topics
- Don't promote competitors

## Review Guidelines

Reviews must follow these rules:

- **No Spam:** No promotional content or links
- **No Conflicts of Interest:** Don't review your own business or competitors
- **No Offensive Content:** No profanity, discrimination, or harassment
- **No Personal Info:** Don't share phone numbers, addresses, or emails
- **Be Truthful:** Only review businesses you've actually used

## Editing or Deleting Reviews

- You can edit your review anytime from your profile
- To delete a review, go to your reviews and click "Delete"
- Edited reviews show an "edited" badge

## Responding to Business Replies

Business owners can respond to your review. You can:

- Reply to their response
- Update your review if the business addressed your concerns
- Report inappropriate responses

## Reporting Inappropriate Reviews

If you see a review that violates guidelines:

1. Click the "Report" button on the review
2. Select the reason for reporting
3. Our team will investigate within 48 hours

## Questions?

Contact support if you need help with reviews or have concerns about content moderation.
